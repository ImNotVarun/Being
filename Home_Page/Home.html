About Transcendental Meditation

Transcendental Meditation, or TM, is a technique for avoiding distracting thoughts and promoting a state of relaxed awareness. It is practiced for 15–20 minutes twice per day while sitting with one's eyes closed.

TM is one of the most widely practiced, researched, and effective methods of self-development. It has been scientifically validated by over 600 research studies conducted at more than 250 independent universities and research institutions worldwide.

The technique involves the use of a sound or mantra, which is silently repeated in the mind, allowing the individual to transcend ordinary thinking and experience a state of pure consciousness. This state is known as transcendental consciousness or the fourth state of consciousness, which is distinct from waking, dreaming, and deep sleep.
