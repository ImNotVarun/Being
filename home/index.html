Transcendental Meditation

Welcome to our Transcendental Meditation website. Here you'll find information about the practice, its benefits, and how to get started.

Transcendental Meditation, or TM, is a technique for avoiding distracting thoughts and promoting a state of relaxed awareness. It is practiced for 15–20 minutes twice per day while sitting with one's eyes closed.
